Maple oat cookies

These Maple oat cookies are perfect for a mid morning snack with a nice cup of tea or coffee.

A perfect treat which is quick and easy to make and fills the home with that lovely smell of baking which is handy if someone is popping over for a visit at short notice but you still want to make them feel extra welcome.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°C

  2. Mix the dry ingredients

    In a medium bowl combine oats, flour, baking soda and salt.

  3. Mix the wet ingredients

    In a large mixing bowl mix the butter and sugar with an electric whisk until pale and fluffy then beat in the maple syrup, egg, vanilla extract and water then combine the oat mixture with this bowl.

  4. Bake

    Make little 1 inch balls from the dough and flatten them into cookie shapes on parchment lined baking sheets. Bake for about 11 minutes or until edges are golden.

  5. Serve

    Let the cookies cool for 2 minutes to set then move to wire racks to cool completely or tuck in whilst they ar still a bit warm and gooey!.
