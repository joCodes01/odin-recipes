Banana bread recipe

This Banana bread is delicious; you can't beat it with a nice cup of tea or coffee.

Our bananas are grown locally by a lady called Pam who works hard and delivers them to us weekly so we try not to waste any. If they are over-ripe, this is the best time to make some banana bread!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 over-ripe bananas
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • ¾ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°C

  2. Mix the wet ingredients

    In a large mixing bowl, add mashed bananas, melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla essence.

  3. Mix the dry ingredients

    In a smaller bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Add this mixture to the larger bowl with the wet ingredients and combine.

  4. Bake

    Pour mixture into a loaf pan. Bake for roughly 1 hour, or until a knife comes out clean.

  5. Serve

    cool on a rack for 15 minutes then slice and serve.
